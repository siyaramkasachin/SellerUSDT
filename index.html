What is Tether?

Tether (USDT) is a type of cryptocurrency known as a stablecoin. Unlike most cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin or Ethereum, which can experience significant price volatility, stablecoins are designed to maintain a stable value by pegging their value to another asset or a basket of assets.
